Travel Resources

Essential Travel Documents

  • Valid Passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Visa (if required for destination country)
  • Travel Insurance documents
  • Flight tickets & hotel booking confirmations
  • ID proofs (Driving license, National ID, etc.)
  • Emergency contact numbers
  • Copies of important documents (both physical & digital)

Guides & Checklists

Pre-Trip Packing Checklist

  • Weather-appropriate clothing
  • Toiletries & personal hygiene items
  • Medicines & first aid kit
  • Travel adapters & chargers
  • Copies of documents & emergency cash

Travel Safety Tips

  • Split money & keep in multiple places
  • Avoid unsafe neighborhoods at night
  • Learn basic local phrases
  • Share itinerary with friends/family

Cultural Etiquette Guide

  • Respect dress codes in religious places
  • Learn basic greetings in the local language
  • Follow tipping practices where applicable
  • Be mindful of local laws & traditions

Tools & Downloads

  • Currency Converter: Check real-time exchange rates before or during travel.
  • Weather & Climate Check: Stay updated on conditions at your destination before departure.
  • Offline Maps: Download Google Maps or Maps.me for offline navigation.
